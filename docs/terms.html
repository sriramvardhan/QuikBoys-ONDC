Order Policy

QuikBoys provides hyperlocal last-mile delivery services across Hyderabad and Bangalore through the ONDC network.

Order Acceptance

Orders are accepted 24/7. Confirmation is sent via ACK response on the ONDC network within 30 seconds.

Service Area

Currently operational in Hyderabad IT Corridor (Kondapur, Gachibowli, Hitech City) and select Bangalore clusters.

Delivery SLA

Immediate: 45–90 min · Same Day: by 9 PM · Next Day: by 12 PM next business day.

Order Tracking

Real-time tracking available via ONDC /track endpoint and WhatsApp updates throughout delivery lifecycle.

Refund Policy

Refunds are processed based on the nature of the issue and delivery outcome.

Scenario Refund Eligibility Timeline
Order not picked up Full Refund Within 24 hours
Item damaged in transit Full Refund Within 48 hours of complaint
Wrong item delivered Full Refund Within 48 hours of complaint
Delayed delivery (>2x SLA) Partial Refund Within 48 hours
Customer cancelled before pickup Full Refund Within 24 hours
Customer cancelled after pickup No Refund N/A

Refunds are credited to the original payment method via Cashfree. UPI refunds: 2–4 hours. Card refunds: 5–7 business days.

Grievance Redressal (IGM)

QuikBoys follows the ONDC Issue & Grievance Management (IGM) framework for all complaints and disputes.

Raise an Issue

Via ONDC /issue endpoint, WhatsApp on +91 89045 38989, or email grievance@quikboys.com.

Response SLA

Initial acknowledgement within 1 hour. Resolution within 48 hours for standard issues.

Escalation

Unresolved issues escalate to ONDC network grievance officer after 48 hours.

Issue Categories

ITEM (wrong/damaged), FULFILLMENT (late/missing), PAYMENT (refund disputes), IGM (platform issues).

Grievance Officer: Balaji Chennupati · grievance@quikboys.com · Response within 48 hours.
